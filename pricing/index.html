Common Questions

Questions About
Our Programs & Pricing

Are there long-term contracts?

We don't lock clients into long-term contracts. Our programs are month-to-month after the initial 3-month commitment period. The reality is that clients stay because the results keep coming — our 97% retention rate is proof. We don't need to trap anyone in a contract to keep them.

What does the setup fee cover?

The setup fee covers the initial build work: website design and development, campaign setup, CRM configuration, automation workflows, GBP optimization, and AI Partner training. It's a one-time cost for the foundation that everything else builds on.

When do I start seeing results?

Google Ads campaigns are typically live within 2 weeks of onboarding and can generate leads from the first week. The website launches within 45 days. SEO results build from months 3–6 onward. By the 6-month mark, most clients are seeing meaningful growth across multiple channels simultaneously.

How much should I budget for ad spend on top of the program fee?

Green Accelerator clients typically invest $1,000–$2,500/month in Google Ads. SAE clients invest $2,000–$5,000/month across Google and Meta. Your specific recommendation will be based on your market size, competition level, and growth targets — we cover this during your strategy call. Ad spend goes directly to Google/Meta; we don't mark it up.

Which program is right for me?

If you're doing $300K–$750K/year and want to grow aggressively, Green Accelerator is your starting point. If you're at $750K+ and want to become the dominant brand in your market with the full program, Service Area Expert is the right choice. Not sure? Book a strategy call and we'll give you an honest recommendation based on your business.

Do you work with new landscaping companies?

We focus on businesses that are already established with real revenue and operational capacity. If you're brand new (first year, minimal revenue), we'd typically recommend building the business foundation first and coming back when you're ready to scale. We want every client to succeed — and that means being honest about fit. Read our Good Fit page →
