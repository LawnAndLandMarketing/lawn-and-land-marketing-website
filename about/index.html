Our Story

We Didn't Fall Into This Industry.
We Chose It — and Never Looked Back.

Before Lawn & Land Marketing, founder Matt Foreman ran Shoot To Thrill Media — a traditional full-service agency serving clients across multiple industries. He was good at it. But he kept noticing something: the lawn care and landscaping companies he worked with had a massive marketing problem that generic agencies were getting completely wrong.

Most marketing agencies treat lawn care like it's the same as any other local service business. They apply the same templates, the same keyword strategies, the same ad copy they use for plumbers and roofers. And they wonder why results are mediocre.

The green industry isn't generic. Seasonality, service area complexity, crew-based operations, high ticket commercial vs. residential splits, the Google Maps vs. organic vs. paid balance — it takes industry-specific expertise to do this right. Matt knew it. He shut down the general agency and went all-in.

Lawn & Land Marketing launched with one promise: serve only lawn care and landscaping companies, and serve them better than anyone else. That focus compounded into an agency that hit $1 million in annual recurring revenue in January 2026, with a 97% client retention rate that speaks louder than any pitch deck.

Meet Matt Foreman

Founder & CEO — Lawn & Land Marketing

Matt Foreman has been in digital marketing since before it had that name. He's seen the industry go through every evolution — from SEO keyword stuffing to Core Web Vitals, from Facebook's heyday to the AI revolution happening right now.

What set Matt apart wasn't just the technical skills — it was the business instinct. After building and running Shoot To Thrill Media, he recognized a gap: lawn care and landscaping companies were being underserved by agencies that didn't understand their world. The seasonality. The crew economics. The way a homeowner decides which lawn care company to trust.

He started Lawn & Land Marketing to fix that. Five years later, it's a $1M+ agency with 52 active clients, a team of 15 specialists, and a 97% retention rate that reflects one simple truth: when you actually deliver results, clients don't leave.

Matt lives in the Tampa Bay area with his wife Jill and their two kids. He's the kind of founder who still takes sales calls himself — because he believes that staying close to clients is how you keep a company sharp.
