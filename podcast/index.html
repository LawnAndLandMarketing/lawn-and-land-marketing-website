Questions About
Mow Money

What is the Mow Money podcast about?

Mow Money is a podcast for lawn care and landscaping business owners who want to grow. Host Matt Foreman covers digital marketing (SEO, Google Ads, Meta Ads), website design, lead generation, marketing automation, and the business systems that help green industry companies scale to 7 figures and beyond.

Is this podcast only for large landscaping companies?

No — episodes are relevant from solo operators doing $200K up to established companies pushing $5M+. The tactics apply at every stage; we flag when something is more relevant to where you are now vs. where you're going.

Is the podcast free to listen to?

Yes, completely free. Every episode is available on Spotify, Apple Podcasts, and YouTube at no cost. We also publish full blog posts for most episodes — you can find those in the blog.

How is Mow Money different from other marketing podcasts?

100% green industry focus, no exceptions. We don't apply generic marketing advice to landscaping — we build strategy specifically for how homeowners and commercial clients find and hire lawn care and landscaping companies. It's a different buyer and a different market.

Is there a companion resource to go with the podcast?

Yes — Matt's free book, Mow Money, Mow Problems, covers the full marketing system in depth. It's the companion playbook to the podcast and it's free to download.

What if I want help implementing what I learn?

That's what we're here for. If you want a team to build and run the system Matt talks about on the podcast, book a free strategy call. No pitch — just a real conversation about your market and what it would take to dominate it.
