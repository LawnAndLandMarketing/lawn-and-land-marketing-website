When someone searches "landscaping company near me," the first 3 results they see aren't websites — they're Google Business Profiles. The Maps pack gets more clicks than the organic results below it. If you're not in the top 3, you're invisible.

Most landscaping companies have a GBP but haven't touched it since they set it up. Sparse photo galleries. Outdated hours. No posts. 8 reviews from 3 years ago. That profile is losing you leads every single day to competitors who've invested in it. Our GBP management program optimizes every element Google uses to rank local profiles — and then keeps it fully maintained, active, and growing every month. Reviews, posts, Q&A, photos, service categories, citations — everything that signals to Google that you're the most relevant, active, and trustworthy landscaping company in the area.

◎

Full Profile Optimization

Business name, categories (primary + secondary), service areas, hours, phone, website, attributes, services menu, and business description — all optimized with keyword-rich, accurate information that signals relevance to Google's local ranking algorithm.

✦

Review Generation & Management

Automated review request sequences after every completed job. Personalized responses to every review — positive and negative — within 24 hours. We monitor your review profile daily and escalate issues that need immediate attention.

▷

Weekly GBP Posts

Fresh content posted to your GBP every week — project showcases, seasonal offers, tips, team updates. Active posting signals engagement to Google and gives homeowners browsing your profile additional social proof and reasons to call.

◆

Photo & Video Gallery Management

We maintain an active, updated photo gallery with recent project photos. Profiles with 100+ photos get significantly more profile views. We coordinate photo collection from your team and ensure your gallery showcases your best work.

◈

Q&A Management

The Questions & Answers section on GBP is often ignored and filled with unanswered questions — or worse, incorrect answers from random people. We seed it with high-value Q&As, monitor for new questions, and respond promptly to ensure accurate information.

◐

Citation Building & Consistency

Your NAP (name, address, phone) must be consistent across every business directory on the web. We audit and fix citations across 50+ directories — Yelp, HomeAdvisor, BBB, Angi, and more — because inconsistencies hurt local rankings.

◑

Monthly GBP Insights Reporting

Monthly reports covering your GBP performance: searches, profile views, phone calls, direction requests, and website clicks — tracked over time so you see your Maps presence growing every month.

GBP Audit
