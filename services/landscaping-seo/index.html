Common Questions

What Landscaping Business Owners
Ask About SEO

How long does landscaping SEO take to see results?

Most clients see measurable ranking improvements within 60–90 days. Significant traffic increases typically happen around the 4–6 month mark. SEO compounds — the results you build in month 3 pay dividends in month 12 and beyond. This is a long-term asset, not a quick fix. Clients who stay with the program see their leads from organic traffic increase every quarter.

What keywords do you target for landscaping companies?

We target a combination of service keywords ("landscaping company [city]", "lawn care near me"), intent-specific phrases ("get a landscaping estimate"), and long-tail service area keywords across every city and town in your target market. We also aggressively target Google Maps rankings through local SEO strategies that complement organic search.

Do I need SEO if I'm already running Google Ads?

Yes. Paid ads deliver immediate leads but stop the moment you stop paying. SEO builds organic rankings that generate free traffic indefinitely. The smartest strategy is running both — ads for immediate leads, SEO for long-term dominance. Most of our top-performing clients do exactly this, and watch their cost-per-lead decrease over time as organic traffic grows.

How is your landscaping SEO different from general SEO services?

We only serve lawn care and landscaping companies. That means we already know which keywords convert in your industry, what content Google rewards for landscaping queries, how seasonal search patterns affect your strategy, and what your competitors are doing wrong. A general SEO agency has to figure this out from scratch every time — and they usually don't.

Can I get SEO without a website redesign?

If your current site has solid technical foundations, yes — we can execute an SEO program on top of it. That said, if your site is slow, not mobile-friendly, or built on a platform that limits optimization (like many DIY builders), we'll be honest with you: there's a ceiling to what we can achieve without addressing the foundation. We'll tell you upfront if a redesign would significantly accelerate results.

Is SEO included in your programs, or is it a separate service?

SEO is a core component of both our Green Accelerator and Service Area Expert programs. It's not a separate line item — it's built into the complete marketing system we run for your business. See program details →
