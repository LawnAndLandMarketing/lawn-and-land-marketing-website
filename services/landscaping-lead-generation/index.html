Common Questions

What Landscaping Business Owners
Ask About Lead Generation

How does lead generation work for landscaping companies?

The most effective landscaping lead generation combines multiple channels: SEO generates organic leads at zero incremental cost over time. Google Ads generates immediate, high-intent leads from active searchers. Meta Ads create awareness and demand with homeowners before they start searching. A conversion-optimized website turns that traffic into estimate requests. And automation ensures every lead is followed up instantly — because speed-to-response is one of the biggest determinants of close rate in the green industry. We coordinate all of these into one system.

How long does it take to see results from a lead generation program?

It depends on the channels in your program. Google Ads and Meta Ads generate leads immediately — usually within the first week of launch. SEO is a 3–6 month build before significant organic traffic kicks in, but compounds indefinitely after that. Most clients see a meaningful increase in estimate requests within 30–60 days of program launch. The full system typically shows its best performance at the 3–6 month mark when paid campaigns are optimized and organic rankings are building.

What makes your lead generation different from buying leads from HomeAdvisor or Angi?

Night and day difference. Purchased leads from HomeAdvisor and Angi are shared with 3–5 competitors simultaneously — you're in a race-to-the-bottom on price the moment the lead comes in. Our lead generation system brings leads directly to you, exclusively. They come from your brand, your website, your ads. The homeowner chose you — not a marketplace. Exclusive leads convert at dramatically higher rates and don't commoditize your services the way shared lead platforms do. The upfront investment is higher, the ROI is significantly better.

How do you track which leads come from which channel?

Full attribution tracking is part of every program. We use UTM parameters, call tracking numbers, and form source tracking to identify whether a lead came from organic search, Google Ads, Meta Ads, or direct traffic. Every month, we report lead volume and cost-per-lead by channel so you know exactly where your marketing dollars are performing — and where to invest more.

Do I need to run all the channels at once?

No — we build programs based on your budget and growth goals. Green Accelerator clients typically start with website, SEO, and one paid channel. Service Area Expert clients run the full multi-channel system. We always recommend starting with Google Ads (immediate leads) while SEO builds (long-term leads), and adding Meta Ads once the foundation is strong. The sequence matters — we'll walk you through what makes sense for your specific situation. See our programs →

What service areas do you work in?

We work with landscaping and lawn care companies across the United States. Our client base spans small regional markets, mid-size cities, and major metros. The strategies scale from a one-man operation targeting a 20-mile radius to multi-crew operations targeting multiple cities and service areas. We customize every campaign to your specific geography and competition level.
