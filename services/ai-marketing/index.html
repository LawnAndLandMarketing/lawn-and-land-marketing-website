Common Questions

What Landscaping Business Owners
Ask About AI Partner

What is the AI Partner program and how is it different from ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is a general-purpose AI that knows nothing about your business. Our AI Partner is a custom GPT trained on your specific company — your services, pricing, sales process, customer Q&A, call transcripts, and competitive positioning. When you ask it "write a proposal for a commercial mowing contract for a 5-acre property," it gives you an answer grounded in your actual pricing and process — not a generic template. The difference is like having an AI employee who's been at your company for years versus a temp who showed up today.

Do I need to be tech-savvy to use my AI partner?

Not at all. The interface is as simple as texting. You type a question or request in plain English — "help me write a follow-up email to a customer who didn't respond to my estimate" — and you get a ready-to-use response back. We onboard your team and show them the highest-value use cases. Most clients are getting real ROI from it within the first week of use.

Is the AI Partner program available as a standalone service?

The AI Partner is included as a standard benefit of our Green Accelerator and Service Area Expert programs — it's not sold as a standalone service. We build it as part of the broader marketing system because the AI becomes significantly more powerful when it's connected to your active campaigns, SEO content strategy, and lead generation data. See program details →

What data do you need to train my custom AI partner?

We start with the basics: your service list and pricing, your service area, your typical customer profile, your brand voice and tone, and any existing marketing materials. As we work together, we add call transcripts (from sales calls and Fathom recordings), customer review content, and specific FAQs your team deals with regularly. The more context we give it, the more useful it becomes — but it's valuable from day one.

Can my entire team use it, or just me?

Your entire team can use it. The custom GPT is deployed through ChatGPT's Teams or custom interface, so you can give access to your office manager, sales team, field supervisors — anyone who needs to generate communications, look up service info, or get quick marketing support. Most clients see the biggest ROI when the person handling customer communications (not just the owner) is using it daily.

What's coming next for the AI Partner program?

We're actively building integrations between the AI Partner and marketing automation, CRM data, and ad campaign performance — so the AI has real-time context on your business performance when giving recommendations. We're also developing AI-assisted SEO content workflows that cut blog production time dramatically while maintaining quality. Clients in the program today will have access to these features as they roll out.
