Founder & CEO

Matt Foreman

Matt built Lawn & Land Marketing on a simple premise: the green industry deserved a marketing agency that actually understood lawn care and landscaping — not a generalist agency that happened to take on a few landscaping clients.

Before founding L&L, Matt ran Shoot To Thrill Media, a full-service digital marketing agency. After repeatedly discovering that specialized, industry-specific marketing dramatically outperformed generic campaigns, he made the decision to go all-in on the green industry exclusively.

That focus paid off. L&L hit $1M annual run rate in January 2026, serves 52+ clients across the US with 97% retention, and is growing every quarter. Matt leads strategy, sales, and continues to be directly involved with every client's program.

How We Operate

The Standards We Hold
Every Day for Every Client

◉

Specialization Over Scale

We'll never take on 500 clients to hit a revenue number. The green industry focus is non-negotiable — because it's exactly what makes our results possible. Generalist agencies can't compete with the depth of knowledge we've built from working exclusively in lawn care and landscaping.

◎

Relationships, Not Accounts

We operate on the Joey Coleman "Never Lose a Customer Again" philosophy. The first 100 days of a client relationship determine whether they stay for years. We invest heavily in onboarding, communication, and delivering early wins that build the trust that leads to 97% retention.

◐

Ownership Mentality

Every team member is trained to think about client results like they own the business. It's not "I managed the campaign." It's "I grew this client's estimate requests by 3x and here's how." That ownership is what drives the outcomes our clients actually care about.

✦

Transparency Always

We don't hide behind vanity metrics. If a channel isn't working, we tell the client and pivot. Monthly reports show real numbers — leads generated, cost per lead, estimated revenue impact. You always know exactly what's happening with your program.
